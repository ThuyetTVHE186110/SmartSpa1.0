What to Expect

  • Consultation to determine your desired look
  • Thorough cleansing of natural lashes
  • Application of premium synthetic lashes
  • Aftercare instructions and tips

Preparation Tips

  • Come with clean, makeup-free lashes
  • Avoid caffeine before your appointment
  • Wear comfortable clothing
  • Remove contact lenses if possible

Aftercare

  • Avoid water contact for 24 hours
  • Don't use oil-based products near eyes
  • Brush lashes daily with provided brush
  • Schedule regular fills every 2-3 weeks

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do lash extensions last?

With proper care, lash extensions can last 2-3 weeks before requiring a fill.

Are lash extensions safe?

Yes! Our certified technicians use premium, safe materials and follow strict hygiene protocols.

Can I wear makeup with lash extensions?

Yes, but we recommend avoiding oil-based products and mascara on the extensions.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

Come with clean, makeup-free lashes and avoid caffeine before your appointment.
