Product Description

Our Professional Lash Cleanser is specifically designed for use with eyelash extensions. This gentle yet effective formula helps maintain the longevity of your lash extensions while keeping them clean and beautiful.

The oil-free formula ensures that the adhesive bond remains strong while effectively removing makeup, dirt, and debris. Regular use helps prevent protein buildup and maintains the health of your natural lashes.

Ingredients

Purified Water, Decyl Glucoside, Sodium Lauroyl Sarcosinate, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, PEG-150 Distearate, Polysorbate 20, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Disodium EDTA, Citric Acid

Free from: Oil, Parabens, Sulfates, Artificial Colors

How to Use

  1. Pump cleanser onto a clean brush or lint-free applicator
  2. Gently work the cleanser through lashes from base to tip
  3. Rinse thoroughly with water
  4. Pat dry gently with a clean towel
  5. Use morning and night for best results
