The Ultimate Guide to Lash Extensions: Everything You Need to Know

8 min read 1.2k views 8 comments

Lash extensions have revolutionized the beauty industry, offering a semi-permanent solution to achieve the long, full lashes of your dreams. In this comprehensive guide, we'll cover everything you need to know before getting lash extensions.

What Are Lash Extensions?

Eyelash extensions are semi-permanent fibers that are attached to your natural eyelashes to make them look longer, fuller, and darker. Each synthetic, silk, or mink extension is attached to a single natural eyelash using a semi-permanent glue.

Types of Lash Extensions

  • Classic Lashes: One extension per natural lash
  • Volume Lashes: Multiple lightweight extensions per natural lash
  • Hybrid Lashes: Combination of classic and volume techniques

The Application Process

The application process typically takes 1.5-2 hours for a full set. During this time, you'll lie down with your eyes closed while a lash artist carefully applies the extensions one by one.

Aftercare Tips

Avoid water and steam for the first 24-48 hours

Don't use oil-based products near your eyes

Brush your lashes daily with a clean spoolie

Schedule regular fills every 2-3 weeks
